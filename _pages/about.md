I am currently a third-year Ph.D. student at the Data Science Academy, **The University of Hong Kong (HKU)**, advised by Prof. [Kai Han](#). Prior to this, I received my Master's degree in Computer Technology from Tsinghua University in 2023, advised by Prof. [Hai-tao Zheng](#), and my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from Nanjing University in 2019. 

My research interests primarily lie in **Large Language Models (LLMs), Reasoning, and Post-Training**. I am particularly focused on enhancing the logical reasoning capabilities of large models on complex tasks, as well as continual learning during post-training.
